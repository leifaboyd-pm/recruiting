Founder & Servant Leader

Dan Trinidad

CEO & Founder · Partners Mortgage

Dan Trinidad founded Partners Mortgage in 1988 with a simple, unwavering belief: everyone matters. Customers matter, referral partners matter, the community matters, and above all — the people on his team matter. That philosophy hasn't shifted in 37 years, and it's the foundation everything at Partners is built on.

Dan entered the industry as a loan officer, working his way up to top producer at a Savings and Loan institution in Saratoga, California, before making the leap to start Partners. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from UC Irvine and has spent every year since building a company where, as he puts it, "I was firm that our philosophy would be 'You Matter.' It refers to everyone we come into contact with."

Under his leadership, Partners has funded over 60,000 loans and become one of the most respected independent mortgage companies in the country. Dan's servant leadership style — leading from the front, investing in people, and measuring success by the impact on others — has attracted a team that shares those values deeply.

Partners For A Cure

Motivated by the loss of his wife to cancer, Dan founded Partners For A Cure in 2011. What started as 20 teammates raising awareness has grown into over $1 million raised for the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society — a testament to what a leader can mobilize when they genuinely care about people.
